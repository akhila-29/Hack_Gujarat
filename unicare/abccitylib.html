Welcome to ABC CITY LIBRARY

We are here to help everyone in need

ABC Donations is a registered non-profit organisation registered under the Societies Registration Act in India. We work with over 900 Indian professionals. We are guided by a Governing Body composed of experienced Indian practitioners, academics and administrators. Founded in 1999 by a pediatrician, Dr. Shamir Chandi, ABC Donations is located in Kolkata (former Calcutta), West Bengal, India and runs operations in the states of West Bengal, Jharkhand and Odhisha , assisting about 7 million people. We have been recipient of prestigious awards and recognitions in India and around the world..
