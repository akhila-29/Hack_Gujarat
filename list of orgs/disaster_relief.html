About Disaster Relief

Disaster relief means fast aid provided for alleviating the suffering of domestic disaster victims. From earlier times, disaster relief is considered a local responsibility of the federal government to provide assistance, when local and state relief capacities are exhausted.

There are a lot of people who need help out there and we should try our best from our side to make sure we do that.
